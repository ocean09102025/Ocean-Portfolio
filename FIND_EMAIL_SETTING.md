You're on the right page! The email recipient setting is usually found in one of these places:

## Option 1: Check the "Overview" Tab
1. Click on the **"Overview"** tab (at the top, next to "Settings")
2. Look for a section showing where emails are sent
3. You should see the recipient email address there

## Option 2: Check the "Workflow" Tab
1. Click on the **"Workflow"** tab (at the top)
2. Look for email actions or notification settings
3. The recipient email should be listed there

## Option 3: Check Templates (if you see it)
1. In the "Settings" tab, you saw "Template" field
2. If you click on templates, you might find email configuration there
3. The recipient email is usually set when configuring email notifications

## Quick Steps:
1. Click **"Overview"** tab first
2. Look for text like "Send to:" or "Email to:" or recipient email
3. If not there, click **"Workflow"** tab
4. Look for email/notification settings

## If You Still Can't Find It:
- The recipient email might be set at the **Project level** (not form level)
- Click on "MY FIRST PROJECT" in the left sidebar
- Then check the project settings for email configuration
